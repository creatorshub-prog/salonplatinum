About Salon Platinum

Where craft meets confidence.

Salon Platinum is a boutique hair studio located in the heart of Virginia Beach, dedicated to delivering high-end color, extension, and styling services that are as unique as you are.


As a BELLAMI VIP Exclusive Salon — one of a select few in the Hampton Roads area — we're certified in the most advanced hair extension methods on the market. Our stylists specialize in color transformations, from sun-kissed balayage to full blonding, and everything in between.
